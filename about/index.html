About Me | CARRIGANREILLY.COM

My name is Carrigan Reilly!

(they/them)

I am a 21 year old non-binary artist/illustrator along with a Game Art and Design major at the Columbus College of Art and Design! A lot of work focuses on character concepts, design, and animation, but I also double as a programmer in HTML/JS and C#. I realized the best way for me to merge and combine these two passions into the best of each would be to make my own portfolio! So I went the extra mile and made my entire portfolio website from scratch and hosted it through Netlify and Github. The Games section, which links out to Itch.io, also encapsulates this feeling. Even from the shoddy prototypes, knowledge learned along the way makes it something I am glad to say was made by me. All of my projects help me grow and improve my skills each step of the way. Coding is something I really enjoy on a very visceral level, it is a lot less creatively inclined and a lot more like solving a really cool puzzle box, and that scratches a really nice itch for me. I am a 20 year old non-binary artist with a focus on character design and 2D animation! I'm currently a junior and a Game Art and Design major at the Columbus College of Art and Design
All of the work I have ever made, no matter how old, is something that I am proud to have made or to have learned from. Even if I have to cut it from my professional portfolio, it was still a learning experience I treasure. I think my entire site encapsulates that feeling.
